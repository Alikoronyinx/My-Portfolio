Onyinyechi Alikor

Product Designer

I'm a Product Designer who enjoys solving real problems through simple, human-centered design.

I got into design because I kept noticing how many digital products frustrate users — and I wanted to understand why, and how to fix it.

Through internships, design challenges, and real projects, I've learned to always start with users. I rely on research and feedback to guide my decisions — not guesswork.

Design Philosophy

"Good design is the kind you don't have to think about — it just works naturally."

I'm looking to join a product team where I can build useful products that improve people's lives while also helping the business grow.

Design Process

How I Think Through Design

01

Discover

Every project starts by understanding the users, the product, and the problem. I ask questions, listen closely, and uncover what truly needs to be solved.

02

Design

I turn ideas into clear, usable interfaces focused on structure, usability, and visual clarity across web and mobile products.

03

Refine

Through feedback and iteration, I refine designs to ensure they work in real use, not just in Figma.

04

Deliver

I take designs from concept to final delivery, keeping them consistent, scalable, and easy for teams to use and build on.
