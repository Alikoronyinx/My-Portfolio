About Me

Crafting experiences,
one pixel at a time.

I'm a passionate Product Designer with a deep love for solving complex problems through human-centered design. My journey began with a curiosity about why digital products fail users — and a determination to change that.

Through internships, design challenges, and real-world projects, I've built a methodology that anchors every design decision in user research. I believe great design is invisible — it simply works.

My goal is to join a product-driven team where I can build products that positively impact millions while bridging the gap between business goals and user needs.

Design Philosophy

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works. Every decision should serve the user, not the designer's ego."

My Design Process

A structured, user-centered approach — from discovery to delivery.

1

Discover

Stakeholder interviews & market research to understand the problem space

2

Research

Interviews, surveys, competitive analysis & persona creation

3

Define

Synthesize insights into problem statements & success metrics

4

Ideate

Brainstorm solutions, sketches, user flows & IA

5

Design

Wireframes, high-fi mockups, prototypes & design systems

6

Test & Iterate

Usability testing, feedback loops & accessibility checks
