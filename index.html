About Me

Crafting experiences,
one pixel at a time.

I'm a Product Designer who enjoys solving real problems through simple, human-centered design.

I got into design because I kept noticing how many digital products frustrate users — and I wanted to understand why, and how to fix it.

Through internships, design challenges, and real projects, I've learned to always start with users. I rely on research and feedback to guide my decisions — not guesswork.

Design Philosophy

"Good design is the kind you don't have to think about — it just works naturally."

I'm looking to join a product team where I can build useful products that improve people's lives while also helping the business grow.

How I Work

My Design Process

A structured, user-centered approach — from discovery to delivery.

01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews & market research to understand the full problem space.

02

Research

Interviews, surveys, competitive analysis & persona creation with real users.

03

Define

Synthesize insights into clear problem statements, HMWs & success metrics.

04

Ideate

Brainstorm solutions, rough sketches, user flows & information architecture.

05

Design

Wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, interactive prototypes & design systems.

06

Test & Iterate

Usability testing, feedback loops, accessibility audits & continuous refinement.
